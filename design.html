Arrow: User Observation

The client wanted a device to aid him in the retrieval of objects from underneath furniture. When playing with his puppy, often times tennis balls would roll underneath the coach or dressers and retrieving them was difficult.

The client would lie flat on the ground and use a yard stick to retrieve the tennis balls.

Many pieces of furniture in the client's house had space for the tennis balls to roll under.

Arrow: A Rapid Retriever on Wheels

Arrow (A Rapid Retriever on Wheels) is a device that is able to rescue items that get lost or forgotten under furniture. The Velcro aids in the gathering of tennis balls for those with canine companions.
